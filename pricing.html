Innovation Within Sports

Video Assiatant Refereeing has had a major impact on the world of football. It has not only changed the experience for referees but for players and fans alike. While it has had moments in which it displayed a positive affect to the game, it has also had its downsides as it leads to longer delays and can even have some errors of its own. regardless, it is currently one of the most infleuntial forms of sports technology in the world today.
