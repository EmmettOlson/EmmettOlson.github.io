Introduction of VAR (Video Assistant Referee)

VAR otherwise known as, Video Assistant Referee, has recently been introcuded among the top football leagues in the world. Its purpose is to account for the mistakes that a referee may make during a game. It accounts for mistakes specifically regarding goals, penalty decisions, direct red-card incidents and mistaken identity. More Info Here.
